# ICE Strategy – Interest–Capitalization Elimination in Reducing-Balance Loans

**Author:** Jeffery Samuel R, MBA, First Year  
**Institution:** Adithya School of Business Management, Coimbatore  

## Overview
The **ICE Strategy** is a borrower-side loan repayment optimization framework designed to reduce total interest in reducing-balance loans through disciplined payment timing. It works without altering loan terms, EMIs, or interest rates.

## Core Principles
- **Interest is Time-Dependent:** Early payments reduce total interest.
- **Timing > Splitting:** Lump-sum or early payments outperform fragmented payments.
- **Neutralize Interest First:** Clear accrued interest to reduce principal effectively.

## Key Insights
- Early payments can save ₹7,000–₹10,000 in standard EMI loans in the first year.
- Prevents interest capitalization during moratorium periods.
- Enhances repayment efficiency within existing loan contracts.
